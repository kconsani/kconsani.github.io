Mathematical Timeline

This timeline records the evolution of the main ideas, with approximate periods, principal constructions, and guiding questions.

Early 1990s
BC-system
Can quantum statistical mechanics reveal fundamental arithmetic structures?
2000–2010
Witt vectors and archimedean period rings
How does the BC-system connect with \(p\)-adic and arithmetic geometry?
2010–2014
Arithmetic Site
Can the finite Riemann sector of the adele class space be realized geometrically?
2014–2017
Scaling Site
How can the archimedean component be incorporated?
2016–2022
Characteristic-one homological algebra
What replaces homological algebra over idempotent algebraic structures?
2018–2024
Segal \(\Gamma\)-rings and spherical algebras
Can one build an absolute geometry independent of characteristic?
2021–2025
Picard and Jacobian geometry of \(\operatorname{Spec}\mathbb Z\)
What is the arithmetic analogue of Picard and Jacobian theories for algebraic curves?
2025–2026
Absolute geometry of \(\operatorname{Spec}\mathbb Z\)
Can absolute geometry recover perfectoid fields, the Fargues--Fontaine curve and local Weil groups?
Current
Towards geometric methods for the Riemann Hypothesis
Can ideas inspired by Weil's proof for function fields be adapted to number fields?
