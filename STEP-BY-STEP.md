# Step-by-step publication instructions

1. Go to https://github.com/ and log in as kconsani.
2. Click the + button in the upper-right corner.
3. Click "New repository".
4. In "Repository name", type exactly: kconsani.github.io
5. Select "Public".
6. Do not add README, license, or .gitignore.
7. Click "Create repository".
8. Click "uploading an existing file" or "Add file" then "Upload files".
9. Upload index.html, README.md, STEP-BY-STEP.md, and the two folders css and images.
10. Scroll down. In "Commit changes", write: Initial website
11. Click "Commit changes".
12. Wait 1--3 minutes.
13. Open https://kconsani.github.io/
